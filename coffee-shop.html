WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP


A lovely and cheap coffee shop , to buy a good cup of "joe" and grab a doughnut,various pastries or mixed nut trail mix as a quick rejuveniting snack. You could also have seat and enjoy your food and drink overlooking the zoo and being one with nature and its peace.

Opening and Closing Times:

  • MONDAYS-FRIDAYS: 7am-2pm
  • SATURDAYS: 8am-1pm
  • SUNDAYS: 8am-11am
